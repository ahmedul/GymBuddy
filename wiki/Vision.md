## The Problem

Going to the gym alone can be:
- **Demotivating** - No one to push you on tough days
- **Isolating** - Missing the social aspect of fitness
- **Inconsistent** - Easy to skip without accountability
- **Boring** - Same routine, no variety or learning

## The Solution

GymBuddy connects gym-goers to work out together, combining the best of:

| App | What We Take |
|-----|--------------|
| **Strava** | Activity tracking, social feed, kudos |
| **Meetup** | Event discovery, group organization |
| **WhatsApp** | Easy coordination, friend groups |

## Target Users

### Primary: The Solo Gym-Goer
- **Age**: 22-40
- **Frequency**: 3-5 times/week
- **Pain Point**: Wants workout partners but doesn't know how to find them
- **Goal**: More enjoyable, consistent workouts

### Secondary: The Fitness Group Organizer
- **Age**: 25-45
- **Role**: Already organizes informal workout groups
- **Pain Point**: Coordination via WhatsApp/text is messy
- **Goal**: Easy scheduling and RSVP tracking

### Tertiary: The Gym Newbie
- **Age**: 18-35
- **Experience**: Just started or returning after a break
- **Pain Point**: Intimidated, unsure what to do
- **Goal**: Learn from others, feel supported

## Why Now?

1. **Post-pandemic fitness boom** - People returning to gyms seeking community
2. **Social fitness trend** - CrossFit, F45, group classes are popular
3. **Coordination fatigue** - Group chats don't scale
4. **Wearables everywhere** - People already track fitness digitally

## Success Metrics

### User Engagement
- **DAU/MAU ratio** > 40%
- **Sessions created** per user per week
- **Session attendance rate** > 70%

### Growth
- **Friend connections** per user
- **Referral rate** (users inviting friends)
- **Gym discovery** (new gyms added)

### Retention
- **Week 1 retention** > 50%
- **Month 1 retention** > 30%
- **Workout streak** (consecutive weeks active)
