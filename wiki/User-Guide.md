## Core Features

### Finding Friends

**From Suggestions:**
1. Go to **Friends** ‚Üí **Add Friends**
2. Browse suggestions (people at your gym)
3. Tap **"+ Add"** to send a friend request

**By Search:**
1. Search by name or email
2. Tap on the person's profile
3. Tap **"Add Friend"**

**Accepting Requests:**
1. Go to **Friends** ‚Üí **Requests**
2. Tap **"Accept"** or **"Decline"**

### Creating a Session

1. Tap **"+"** button or **"Create Session"**
2. Fill in the details:
   - **Title**: "Leg Day with the crew"
   - **Location**: Select from your gyms
   - **Date & Time**: When you'll work out
   - **Duration**: How long (30-180 min)
   - **Capacity**: Max participants (optional)
   - **Visibility**: Who can see/join
3. Add exercises (optional)
4. Tap **"Create Session"**

### Joining a Session

1. Browse sessions in your **Feed**
2. Tap on a session to see details
3. Choose your RSVP:
   - **Going** ‚úì - You'll be there
   - **Maybe** ? - Not sure yet
   - **Skip** ‚úó - Can't make it
4. Get notified of any changes

### Adding Exercises

1. Open a session you created/joined
2. Tap **"Add Exercise"**
3. Enter details:
   - Exercise name
   - Sets √ó Reps (e.g., 4 √ó 10)
   - Notes (optional)
4. Reorder by dragging
5. Tap **"Save"**

### Checking In

## Groups

### Why Create a Group?

- **Regular workout crews** - Same people, recurring sessions
- **Training programs** - 8-week challenge group
- **Gym communities** - All members of your gym

### Creating a Group

1. Go to **Groups** ‚Üí **"+ New Group"**
2. Set group name and description
3. Choose visibility:
   - **Public** - Anyone can discover and request to join
   - **Private** - Invite only
4. Invite initial members
5. Tap **"Create"**

### Managing a Group

As a group admin, you can:
- Add/remove members
- Create sessions for the group
- Edit group info
- Delete the group

## Tips & Best Practices

### For Session Creators

‚úÖ **Do:**
- Set capacity limits for crowded sessions
- Add exercises so partners can prepare
- Message attendees about plan changes
- Check in when you arrive

‚ùå **Don't:**
- Cancel last minute (reschedule instead)
- Set sessions too far in advance (7 days max)
- Create duplicate sessions

### For Participants

‚úÖ **Do:**
- RSVP honestly (update if plans change)
- Arrive on time
- Follow the planned exercises
- Check in to confirm attendance

‚ùå **Don't:**
- RSVP "Going" if you're unsure
- Ghost without updating RSVP
- Join sessions you can't attend

### Building Your Network

1. **Start small** - Invite 1-2 friends
2. **Be consistent** - Regular sessions build community
3. **Mix it up** - Try sessions with new people
4. **Give kudos** - Encourage your workout partners

## Troubleshooting

### Can't find my gym?

1. Try different search terms (full name, abbreviations)
2. Check the location is correct
3. Add it as a custom location if not found

### Session not showing in feed?

1. Check the date filters
2. Ensure you're friends with the creator
3. The session may be private/group-only

### Not receiving notifications?

1. Check phone notification settings
2. Verify in-app notification preferences
3. Ensure app has background refresh enabled

### Can't join a session?

- Session may be at capacity
- You may need to be friends with the creator
- The session may be group-only
